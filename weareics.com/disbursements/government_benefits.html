Helping federal and state governments distribute benefits.

Federal and state governments can distribute benefits (e.g., child support, unemployment insurance, TANIF) using an ICS prepaid card rather than a check.

Funds are immediately available. There are no checks to cash, the cards are usable at many merchant locations and have bill-paying capabilities and they keep funds segregated for easy accountability.
