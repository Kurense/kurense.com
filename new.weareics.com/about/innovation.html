Innovative is not just a part of our name. It is what we do.

Our philosophy is that prepaid cards serve different purposes for different consumers. Our strategy is to develop prepaid product innovations and technological applications that have brand visibility and the ability to capture untapped segments of the electronic payments market.

Technology

Innovative Card Solution's proprietary prepaid technology platform is the backbone of our business. Our platform was custom-built from the ground up for the express purpose of our in-house prepaid card programs and also to license our technology to financial institutions and other prepaid card program managers.

Financial Literacy

Our financial literacy program is an interactive online course designed for consumers to learn about managing money in a controlled-based learning model that offers easy access to detailed textual resources, printable downloads, financial calculators, and other resources

Money Management

Our prepaid card programs are fully integrated with online money management tools and messaging alerts that help consumers learn to manage their money more effectively. These tools are designed to help consumers to set monthly budgets and to financially track budget vs. actual spend on their prepaid card.

Marketing

ICS delivers turnkey prepaid solutions that are fully integrated with your brand. We offer cost effective and complimentary marketing support including design, distribution, and fulfillment services.

In addition, your branded prepaid card solution acts as a mini-billboard for your business each time a consumer or employee uses it.
